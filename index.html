Frequently Asked Questions

If you have other questions or issues, please contact us.

  • What is mobile physical therapy?

    Mobile physical therapy is orthopedic outpatient physical therapy in the comfort of your own home, or even in your office. We come to you to help you get on the road to recovery.

  • Is this a home health practice?

    No, PT Strong Sports Rehab is not a home health agency. If you need skilled nursing, or have a highly complex medical background that requires higher levels of supervision by a physician, then home health is more likely a more appropriate fit.

  • Do you accept insurance?

    PT Strong Sports Rehab operates as a self pay practice, and as such we do not accept any insurance or Medicare. We are more than happy to provide the proper documentation needed to file a claim with your insusrace company.

  • Do you accept Medicare?

    PT Strong Sports Rehab does not accept Medicare at this time.

  • Why would I want to pay out of pocket if I have insurance?

    Most insurance plans are showing higher deductibles and decreasing benefits which results in less quality of care. We are not limited or governed by the restrictions set by insurance companies and so that means you get the one-on-one attention you need and deserve!

  • What areas do you serve?

    We will travel to Palm Beach and northern Broward Counties. If a patient lives outside of our coverage area, special arrangements can be made.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    We understand that life can get busy and that unexpected things happen, and we strive to always do our best to accommodate schedule changes. A scheduled appointment must be cancelled at least 24 hours in advance of the appointment or a full appointment fee will be charged unless the appointment is made up in the same week.
